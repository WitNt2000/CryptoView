Privacy Policy for CryptoView

This application is designed to track cryptocurrency pair prices from exchanges such as Binance, Bitget, and Bybit.

Data Collection and Usage

The app does not collect or store any personal information from users. All settings (e.g., update interval) are stored locally on the device using SharedPreferences and are not shared externally.

Internet Access

The application requests data from public APIs to display cryptocurrency prices. No personal data is sent during these requests.

Auto-start on Boot

To ensure widget functionality, the app uses the RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED permission. This is required to restore scheduled updates after device reboot and does not involve personal data collection.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Continued use of the application implies acceptance of the updated policy.
